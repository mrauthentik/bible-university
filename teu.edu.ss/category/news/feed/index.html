On the 8th March 2024 a ground-breaking ceremony took place to celebrate the beginning of the construction of the Rokon campus gate and fence by Canaan Construction Company. Work has now begun and is due to be completed in June 2024.

TEU is grateful to the Community of St Denys and the Episcopal Churches of South Sudan and Sudan University Partnership for making the funds for this milestone project available.
